# Salvation
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

God’s rescuing work in Christ by which guilty and dying people are delivered from wrath and death and brought into life with Him.  
In Paul’s letters, salvation names the broad saving work God accomplishes in Christ, gives in the present, and brings to completion.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that salvation is God’s rescuing work for people who are perishing and without hope apart from Christ.  
God saves by His mercy and purpose, not by human works.  
This salvation is received through faith in Christ and confession of Him as Lord.  
It brings present rescue and the sure hope of final deliverance through Him.

## 10. Reflection Questions

1. Why is it important that Paul describes salvation as God’s work rather than human achievement?
2. What stands out to you about Paul presenting salvation as rescue now and final rescue still to come?
3. How does Paul’s own example in [1 Timothy 1:15–16](https://biblehub.com/bsb/1_timothy/1.htm) help you understand salvation better?
