# Forgiveness
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

God’s removal of guilt and trespass through Christ so that offenses are no longer counted against His people.  
In Paul’s letters, this pardon is received from God and becomes a pattern for believers toward one another.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that people need forgiveness because they stand guilty in their trespasses and sins.  
God grants that forgiveness in Christ, no longer counting sins against those He pardons.  
This pardon belongs to those made alive with Christ and blessed by His grace.  
Those forgiven by God are called to extend forgiveness to others.
