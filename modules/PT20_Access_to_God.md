# Access to God
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

Access to God refers to the open approach believers now have to the Father through Jesus Christ.  
In Paul’s letters, this access is granted by Christ and enjoyed through faith and the Spirit.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that people once far away from God have been brought near through Christ.  
Through Him believers have access to the Father by one Spirit.  
This access is enjoyed through faith and comes with boldness and confidence.  
Those who have access are no longer strangers but members of God’s household.
