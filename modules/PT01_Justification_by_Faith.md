# Justification by Faith
**Teachings from the Letters of Paul the Apostle**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

Justification refers to being **justified (declared righteous before God)**.  
Paul teaches that this is given by grace (God’s undeserved favor) and received through faith (trust in God and in Jesus Christ), not by works of the Law (the commandments given through Moses).

## 7. Summary

All people are under sin and cannot be justified by the Law.  
God has revealed righteousness apart from the Law through Jesus Christ.  
This righteousness is given by grace and received through faith.  
Those who are justified by faith have peace with God.
