# Condemnation
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

The guilty verdict and liability to judgment that fall on people under God’s righteous sentence.  
In Paul’s letters, this verdict is tied to Adam’s trespass, human guilt, and divine judgment.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that condemnation is the judicial result of sin and belongs to the world under Adam.  
God’s righteous judgment exposes guilt, and Adam’s trespass brought condemnation for all.  
But through Christ’s righteous act, God answers condemnation with justification and life.  
For those who are in Christ Jesus, there is now no condemnation.
