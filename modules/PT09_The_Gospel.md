# The Gospel
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

The gospel is the good news of what God has done in Jesus Christ for salvation.  
In Paul’s letters, the gospel centers on Christ’s death, resurrection, and saving power for all who believe.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that the gospel is God’s good news concerning His Son.  
It centers on Christ’s death for sins and His resurrection.  
The gospel is God’s power for salvation to everyone who believes.  
It must be received, held firmly, and never replaced with a distortion.
