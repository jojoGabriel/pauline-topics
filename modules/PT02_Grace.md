# Grace
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

God’s undeserved favor shown in His saving action toward people who cannot rescue themselves.  
In Paul’s letters, grace is God’s generous action in saving sinners and sustaining believers in Christ.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that grace is God’s undeserved favor toward sinners.  
It is not earned by works but given through Christ and received through faith.  
Grace saves, justifies, and sustains believers.  
It also instructs believers to live godly lives.
