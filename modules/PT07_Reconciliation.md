# Reconciliation
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

The restoration of a broken relationship with God after hostility and alienation have been removed through Christ.  
In Paul’s letters, God acts to bring former enemies back into His favor and fellowship.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that people were alienated from God and stood as His enemies.  
God has acted in Christ to reconcile them to Himself.  
This reconciliation comes through Christ’s death and the blood of His cross.  
Those reconciled now rejoice in God and stand accepted in His presence.
