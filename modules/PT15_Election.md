# Election
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

God’s choice of a people according to His own purpose rather than human merit.  
In Paul’s letters, this choice highlights divine initiative and compassion, not human worthiness.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that election rests in God’s purpose and grace rather than human deserving.  
God chose His people in Christ and chose them for holiness and salvation.  
Election is illustrated in both Israel’s history and the present remnant.  
Because election rests in God’s purpose, it gives strong assurance.
