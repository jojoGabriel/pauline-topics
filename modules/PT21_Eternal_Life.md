# Eternal Life
**Paul’s Teachings by Topic**  
**Volume 1: Salvation and Foundations**  
**A Structured Topical Study from the Letters of Paul the Apostle Using Selected Passages**  
**Text Basis:** Berean Standard Bible (BSB)

---

## 1. Definition

The life God gives in Christ that overcomes death and belongs to the age to come.  
In Paul’s letters, this life is both a present promise and the believer’s future inheritance in Christ.

## 7. Summary

Paul teaches that eternal life is God’s gift in Christ, set over against the death brought by sin.  
God promised this life before time began and brings it through Christ.  
Believers live in hope of eternal life and are called to take hold of it in faith.  
Eternal life is both promised future and certain outcome in Christ.

## 9. Observation Questions

1. According to [Romans 6:22–23](https://biblehub.com/bsb/romans/6.htm), how is eternal life contrasted with the result of sin?
2. What do [Titus 1:2](https://biblehub.com/bsb/titus/1.htm) and [2 Timothy 1:1](https://biblehub.com/bsb/2_timothy/1.htm) show about the promise of eternal life?
3. How do [Titus 3:7](https://biblehub.com/bsb/titus/3.htm) and [1 Timothy 6:12](https://biblehub.com/bsb/1_timothy/6.htm) describe the believer’s relation to eternal life?

## 10. Reflection Questions

1. Why is eternal life described as God’s gift rather than human achievement?
2. What stands out to you about eternal life being a hope we have now and a future we still await?
3. How does eternal life change the way you think about obedience, suffering, and endurance?
